## Content Addressing

Each paper is content-addressed by SHA-256. The `content_address` table in `cmplx_unified.db` maps:
- `hash` -> `canonical_id`
- Content integrity is verifiable by recomputing the hash.

```python
import hashlib
with open('paper-NN.md', 'r') as f:
    h = hashlib.sha256(f.read().encode()).hexdigest()
```
