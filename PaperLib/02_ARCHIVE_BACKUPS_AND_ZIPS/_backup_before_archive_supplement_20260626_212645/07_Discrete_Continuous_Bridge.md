## Definitions

**Discrete trace:**
```text
D = [(0, x0), (1, x1), …, (n, xn)]
```

**Sample-preserving bridge:** continuous function `F` on `[0, n]` with `F(k) = xk` for every integer sample `k`.

**Piecewise-linear construction:** for `t ∈ [k, k+1]`,
```text
a = t − k
F(t) = (1 − a)·xk + a·x(k+1)
```

**MDHG/SpeedLight retraction:** a continuous 24D vector is quantized onto a discrete bin lattice and assigned to a deterministic 2D torus slot. Re-admission is idempotent:
```text
f(f(x)) = f(x)
```

**O3 structural universality:** all 8 chart states close to a Lie conjugate (`L=R`) in ≤3 S3 transposition steps; transport is total via the wrap table.
