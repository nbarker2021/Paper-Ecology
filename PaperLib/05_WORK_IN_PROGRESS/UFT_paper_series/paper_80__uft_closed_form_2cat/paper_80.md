The 8 irreducible gaps are explicit: CKM numerics, particle VOA weights, Higgs mass derivation, Γ72 landing, full Moonshine identification, unbounded Rule 30 non-periodicity, GR EFE identity, cosmogenesis. The UFT is the destination paper of Band B'. The handoff to Band C (Papers 81–100) is via the 8 irreducible gaps. All claims are backed by receipts.
