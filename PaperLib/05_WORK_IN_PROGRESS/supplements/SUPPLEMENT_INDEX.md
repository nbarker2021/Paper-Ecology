## Editing Contract

- Do not use supplements to promote open claims inside the spine.
- Do use supplements to collect evidence, alternative phrasings, receipts, failed routes, and next obligations.
- Each supplement should name which claims are closed, bridge-only, estimate-only, speculative, or external-calibration dependent.
- Each supplement should point back to the spine paper that owns the core statement.
