# Original 80 Publication Expansion Plan

This plan treats the original paper number as the proof-ribbon slot.
The current FLCR series remains a 40-paper publication packaging layer; this file describes the deeper 80-slot original-paper expansion.

Total slots: 80
Source-backed slots: 33
Expansion-needed slots: 47

## Why This Exists

The original `00-32` papers already act as sequential semantic explanations and formal proof carriers.
Extending to `00-79` does not mean inventing arbitrary topics; it means filling the missing higher-lift slots for the same ten action families.


## FLCR-40 And FLCR-41..80 Architecture Note

FLCR-40 is the reconstruction map and trust-removal protocol for the first forty-paper package. It is not expected to carry every receipt again. Its job is to route each grand-claim segment to prior proof owners, analog reconstructions, code/tool routes, comparators, and blockers.

The post-map sequence, FLCR-41 through FLCR-80, is reserved for the literal observer-framework rebuild of Standard Model and adjacent physics domains. These later papers are not merely extra slots; they are where bridge-preview claims from FLCR-31 through FLCR-39 become domain-local derivations with their own receipts, analog routes, comparators, calibration rows, and falsifiers.
