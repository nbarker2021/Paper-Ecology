## Definitions

**Definition 1 (Large-Scale Structure, LSS).** The distribution of galaxies, clusters, filaments, walls, and voids on scales of 100 Mpc and above.

**Definition 2 (Power Spectrum $P(k)$).** The two-point statistic of the density contrast in Fourier space:
$$
P(k) = \langle |\delta_k|^2 \rangle,
$$
where $\delta_k$ is the Fourier transform of $\delta(x) = (\rho(x) - \bar\rho)/\bar\rho$.

**Definition 3 (Baryon Acoustic Oscillation Scale, $r_{\text{BAO}}$).** The characteristic comoving scale $r_{\text{BAO}} \approx 147$ Mpc, representing the distance sound waves travelled in the photon-baryon fluid before recombination.

**Definition 4 (Receipt).** (From Paper 11, Definition 2.1.) A *receipt* is a verifiable record of a carrier state at a specific point in a causal chain.

**Definition 5 (Boundary Repair).** (From Paper 5, Definition 2.1.) A *boundary repair* is an operator that removes a uniform boundary and creates structure; the *repair curvature* measures the local deviation from uniformity.

**Definition 6 (Typed Boundary).** (From Paper 5, Definition 2.1.) A *typed boundary* is a boundary endowed with a conserved charge (the boundary type) and a repair curvature; it is the locus where boundary repair acts.

**Definition 7 (Dark Matter Halo).** A gravitationally bound region of dark matter that has collapsed under its own gravity; the halo mass $M$ is the conserved charge of the boundary type.

**Definition 8 (Growth Rate $f$).** The logarithmic derivative of the linear growth factor $D(a)$ with respect to the scale factor $a$:
$$
f = \frac{d \ln D}{d \ln a}.
$$

**Definition 9 (Gravitational Instability).** The dynamical process by which small density perturbations grow under self-gravity, driving the collapse of matter into halos, filaments, and clusters.

**Definition 10 (Lattice Code Chain).** (From Paper 4, Definition 5.1.) The finite sequence $1 \to 3 \to 7 \to 8 \to 24 \to 72$ derived from the Freudenthal–Tits magic square, providing a combinatorial model for physical structures.
