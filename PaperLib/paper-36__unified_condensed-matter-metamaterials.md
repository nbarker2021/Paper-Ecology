## 2. Definitions

**Materials candidate** — The internal representation of a material generated by the forge (Paper 21).

**Metamaterial** — An engineered structure with geometry designed to produce specific electromagnetic, acoustic, or mechanical properties.

**Photonic crystal** — A periodic dielectric structure that creates band gaps for electromagnetic waves.

**Topological insulator** — A material that is insulating in the bulk but conducting at the surface, with topologically protected edge modes.

**Lattice code chain** — The sequence 1→3→7→8→24→72 derived from the Freudenthal–Tits magic square (Paper 4).

**E6 root system** — The 72 roots of the exceptional Lie group E6.
