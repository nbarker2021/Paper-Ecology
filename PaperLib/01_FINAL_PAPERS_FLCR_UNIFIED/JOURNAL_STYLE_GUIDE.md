# Journal Style Guide

Each FLCR paper should read as a submission-intent manuscript.

Required apparatus:

- Structured contribution statement.
- Claim-evidence table.
- At least three planned figures.
- At least four planned tables.
- Worked example.
- Paper-local nomenclature/glossary.
- Evidence appendix.
- Data/code availability.
- Reviewer checklist.
- Declarations.

Figures may remain formal schematic figures until final graphic design. A figure
is acceptable at this stage when it states the objects, arrows, evidence lanes,
and boundaries needed by a reviewer.
