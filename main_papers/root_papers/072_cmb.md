**Definition 72.3 (Acoustic peak).** A local maximum in the CMB power spectrum arising from standing-wave oscillations in the photon-baryon fluid prior to recombination; first peak at \(\ell \approx 220\).

**Definition 72.5 (Last-scattering surface).** The spherical boundary at recombination (\(z \approx 1100\)) when photons last scattered off free electrons.

**Definition 72.6 (Optical depth).** \(\tau = \int n_e \sigma_T dl\), the line integral of Thomson scattering opacity; identified in FLCR as the local curvature of the boundary repair process.
