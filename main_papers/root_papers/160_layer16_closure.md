## 10. Extended Proof: Layer Integrity

**Lemma 160.B (Layer integrity).** Each layer ℓ = 1,...,16 has the property that its 10 papers form an independent DAG: no paper in Layer ℓ depends on any paper in Layer ℓ' for ℓ' > ℓ (no forward dependencies within the same tier).

Since all papers in Layer ℓ' > ℓ have larger indices than all papers in Layer ℓ, there are no forward dependencies from ℓ to ℓ'. ∎
